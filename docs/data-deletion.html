Hume Data Deletion

Last updated: February 15, 2026

How to Delete Data in the App

  • Delete habits and motivation items directly from app screens.
  • Disable notifications from app settings or device settings.

How to Delete All Data

  • Uninstall Hume from your device, or
  • Clear app storage data from device app settings.

Account Deletion

Hume currently does not require user account creation. There is no account profile to delete in this version.
