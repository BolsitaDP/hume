Hume Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

1. Overview

Hume is a personal habit and motivation app. This policy explains what data is used by the app and how it is handled.

2. Data We Process

  • Habit data you create (title, schedule, completion history).
  • Motivation items you add (links, media references, optional titles).
  • Settings data (language, tone level, notification preferences).

This app does not require account registration in its current version.

3. Storage

App data is stored locally on your device. At this time, Hume does not run a user account backend for syncing personal data to external servers.

4. Notifications

If you grant notification permission, Hume can schedule reminder notifications on your device. You can disable notification permission any time in system settings.

5. Shared Content

If you share a link/media into Hume using your device share menu, that content is used only to create a motivation item in your local app data.

6. Third-Party Services

Hume can open third-party URLs you save (for example YouTube, TikTok, Instagram). Their own privacy policies apply when you visit those platforms.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete content directly inside the app. You can also clear app data or uninstall the app to remove locally stored information.

See Data Deletion for details.

8. Children

Hume is not directed to children under 13.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. The latest version will be posted on this page with an updated date.
